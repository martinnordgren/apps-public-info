# Privacy Policy for SpectralScan

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Overview

SpectralScan is an audio spectrum analyzer app developed by Otsukare AB. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

**SpectralScan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### Microphone Access

SpectralScan requires microphone access to perform real-time audio spectrum analysis.

- Audio data is processed **entirely on your device**
- Audio is analyzed in real-time and **never recorded or stored**
- Audio data is **never transmitted** to any server
- No audio leaves your device

### Saved Measurements

When you save sweep measurements, they are stored **locally on your device only**. This data:
- Remains on your device
- Is never uploaded to any server
- Is never shared with third parties
- Can be deleted at any time by clearing app data

### Analytics and Tracking

SpectralScan does **not** include any:
- Analytics SDKs
- Crash reporting services
- Advertising frameworks
- User tracking

## Third-Party Services

SpectralScan does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

## Children's Privacy

SpectralScan does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
