# Privacy Policy for SpectralScan

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Data Collection

**SpectralScan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### Microphone Access

SpectralScan is using microphone only for audio system analysis. Audio is processed on-device and never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

### Saved Measurements

When you save sweep measurements, they are stored **locally on your device only**. Never shared or uploaded.

### Analytics and Tracking

No analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or third-party SDKs.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
